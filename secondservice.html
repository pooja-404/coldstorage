Smart Air Handling Unit

Our Smart AHU is an advanced air handling unit equipped with an integrated control system. Making it ideal for the places that may not have other BMS (building management systems) and larger buildings where linking to existing BMS is required. Its integrated control system can be operated remotely.

Zeco's Smart AHU is an advanced solution for air handling needs with an integrated control system. It supports a wide range of standard communication protocols, making it suitable for any kind of application. The AHU ensures the best air quality, a comfortable indoor environment, and up to 25% energy and maintenance cost savings. It has real-time monitoring and advanced analytics for 24x7 management, and it is easily accessible through smartphones, tablets, and PCs. The Smart AHU has a compact design and high-efficiency fans and motors with low noise, an EC fan with inbuilt Modbus, and customized solutions based on end-user requirements. It functions efficiently by maintaining a constant room temperature through fan speed modulation, cooling/heating coil actuator modulation, and supply air damper modulation. The Smart AHU is suitable for pharmaceuticals, commercial and residential properties, and offices, and can be integrated with BMS systems.
